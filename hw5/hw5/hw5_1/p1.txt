Forwarding takes place for second and third instruction.

For the second instruction (ld r4, r3, 5), 
	the r3 value from the ex/mem register forwared to input 1 of the alu.

For the thrd instruction addi (r5, r4, 6), 
    the r4 value is forwarded from the mem/wb stage to input 2 of alu.
